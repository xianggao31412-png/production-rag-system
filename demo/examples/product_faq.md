# Aurora Analytics — Support FAQ

## Accounts & Access

**How do I reset my password?**
Open the account settings page, click "Reset password", and follow the link sent
to your registered email address. The link expires after 60 minutes.

**How do I add a teammate to my workspace?**
Workspace owners can invite members from Settings → Team → Invite. Invited users
receive an email and are added with the "Member" role by default.

## Billing

**When am I charged?**
Subscriptions renew on the same calendar day each month. Annual plans are billed
once per year on the anniversary of sign-up.

**What is your refund policy?**
Refunds are processed within 14 business days of an approved request. Monthly
plans are refundable within 7 days of a charge; annual plans are pro-rated.

## Data & Limits

**What is the maximum file size for uploads?**
Individual uploads are capped at 50 MB on the Standard plan and 250 MB on the
Business plan.

**How long is data retained after cancellation?**
Workspace data is retained for 30 days after cancellation, after which it is
permanently deleted.

## Support

**What are your support hours?**
Support is available from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM Eastern Time, Monday through Friday.
Business-plan customers also have access to weekend priority support.
