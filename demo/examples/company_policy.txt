ACME Corporation — Employee Handbook (Excerpt)

1. Paid Time Off
All full-time employees receive 20 days of paid annual leave per calendar year.
Leave accrues monthly and unused days may be carried over up to a maximum of 5
days into the following year. Part-time employees accrue leave on a pro-rata
basis according to their contracted hours.

2. Remote Work
Employees may work remotely up to three days per week with prior approval from
their direct manager. Fully remote arrangements are considered case by case and
must be documented in writing with the People team.

3. Probation Period
New employees serve a probation period of 90 days. During probation, either
party may end the employment relationship with two weeks of notice.

4. Working Hours
Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Core
collaboration hours, when all team members should be reachable, are 10:00 AM to
3:00 PM in the employee's local time zone.

5. Expense Reimbursement
Business expenses must be submitted within 30 days of being incurred. Receipts
are required for any expense over 25 dollars. Approved reimbursements are paid
out with the next scheduled payroll run.

6. Equipment
The company provides a laptop and an external monitor to every new hire.
Hardware refresh is available every three years, or sooner if the device fails.
